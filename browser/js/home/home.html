WatchDog

WatchDog helps guard your laptop or wifi-enabled device when left unattended.

For whatever reason you leave your laptop alone, just have WatchDog monitor for any changes and notify you via text if something suspicious happens such as a change in motion, geolocation or wifi connection. WatchDog will also bark. A lot.
